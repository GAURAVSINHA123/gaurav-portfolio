The Best Web Series According to Me

My top 3 Web Series of all-time.

1. Farzi

A brilliant small-time artist is propelled into the high-stakes world of counterfeiting when he creates the perfect fake currency note.

2. Money Heist

A genius strategist and people with different personalities and abilities fighting an extraordinary variable and engaging in an unprecedented hostage play.

3. Asur: Welcome to Your Dark Side

A former forensic expert returns to join the Central Bureau of Investigation as they investigate the brutal murders of a serial killer in Varanasi.
